Interpretation¶

It is safe to say that the samples from the corpora are not sophisticated, without having to breakdown the individual scores or statistics. The low sophistication is assumed due to the high Frequencies across the plots, higher frequencies also indicate that words from the British National Corpus and the Academic Word List sets have been observed many times; the more a word is used, the less sophisticated it becomes in some aspects.

This low sophistication is not a bad thing, this can also indicate a corpora that prioritizes getting straight to the point. This subreddit discussion is revolving around COVID-19 and the many subjects surrounding it such as vaccines and lockdowns, people would want to make their points clear and understandable.
